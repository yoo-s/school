Project Info


My project for this class will be a virtual pet site where people can make their own accounts and adopt a virtual pet, which they can play with and care for. There will be a forum where logged-in users can post threads about various topics, such as sharing tips on how to play the game or just chatting together and making new friends who also play the game.
Note: Project is subject to change and more info may be added later.
