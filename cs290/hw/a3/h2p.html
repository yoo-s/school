How to Play


Rock Paper Scissors is a simple game where you as a player can select one of three options - rock, paper, or scissors -
against your opponent who selects from the same choices. There are three possible outcomes (other than a tie):

Rock beats scissors
Paper beats rock
Scissors beat paper.

You can play as many times as you like and the scores will be shown at the bottom. Have fun!
